I am a researcher working on large language models, contextual reasoning, and efficient machine learning systems. My recent work focuses on simplifying and optimizing LLM architectures while improving their adaptability across tasks and deployment settings.

## News

- **Dec 2025:** Our paper **“Ipsum Lorem is all you need”**, previously listed as under review, was accepted to **COML 2025** and selected for an **oral presentation**. COML noted that oral presentations represent the **top ~1% of submissions**. The accompanying code is now **open-sourced** on [GitHub](https://github.com/wanishkolodar/ipsum-lorem-all-you-need).
- **Dec 2025:** Our workshop paper **“Ipsum Lorem is all you need for a workshop”**, previously listed as under review, was accepted to the **COML Workshop on Large Language Models (COMLW 2025)**.
- **Dec 2025:** Our paper **“Optimizing Large Language Models for Contextual Reasoning in Multi-Task Environments”**, previously listed as under review, was accepted to **COAI 2025**. The accompanying code is now **open-sourced** on [GitHub](https://github.com/wanishkolodar/optimizing-llms-contextual-reasoning).
